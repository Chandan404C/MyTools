Frequently Asked Questions

📊 What does the word counter measure?
It measures words, total characters, characters without spaces, sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time (based on 200 words per minute).
🔒 Is my text stored anywhere?
No. Everything runs in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server. You can even use the tool offline after the page has loaded.
📏 Is there a text length limit?
There is no imposed limit. The tool handles very large texts limited only by your browser's memory.
🎯 What is the word goal feature?
Set a target word count and a progress bar will track how close you are. Great for essays, blog posts, or reports with a required length.
📱 Does it work on mobile?
Yes! The interface is fully responsive and works on all screen sizes including phones and tablets.
🔤 What are keywords / density?
The keyword density section shows the top 10 most-used words (3+ letters) and the percentage of the total word count each represents — useful for SEO writing.
