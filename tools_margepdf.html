    Frequently asked
    Is there a limit on file size?
    No, as long as your device can handle the files. PDF‑lib runs locally, so it depends on your RAM.
    Can I merge encrypted PDFs?
    Only password‑free PDFs are supported. If a file requires a password, the merge will fail.
    Do you keep my files on a server?
    Never. Everything stays in your browser. Once you close the tab, all traces are gone.
