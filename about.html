Who we are

Making document tools simple & free

ToolHub was born from the frustration of overcomplicated PDF software. We believe powerful tools should be effortless, private, and accessible to everyone.

Our story

Founded in 2022, ToolHub started as a small side project by two developers who needed a quick way to merge PDFs without uploading sensitive files to unknown servers. We realised that most "free" tools are full of ads, limits, or privacy risks.

So we built our own — with end‑to‑end encryption, automatic file deletion, and a clean interface. Today, millions of people use ToolHub to handle documents, images, and office files, all 100% free.
